About Planet English

Founded in 2023 in the heart of Salé, Planet English Association is dedicated to making English accessible to all. We offer engaging, personalized lessons designed for children, teens, and adults. Our mission is to connect our local community to global opportunities by nurturing a love for learning English — one student at a time.

Whether you're just starting or looking to improve your skills, our supportive and welcoming environment helps every learner feel confident. At Planet English, we believe that language is not just a subject — it's a gateway to the world.

Learn. Connect. Grow.

In Salé, English education begins early in private schools, with public schools only recently incorporating it into their curriculum. While most students have access to English through online resources, fluency remains a challenge for many. Parents often seek to help their children become fluent but struggle to find a supportive community for them to grow in.

Whether you're just starting your English journey or looking to advance your skills, our engaging and personalized lessons ensure that every learner feels confident and supported. At Planet English, we believe language is more than just a subject — it’s the gateway to new possibilities.
